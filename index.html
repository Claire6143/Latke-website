Latke recipe

Latkes are really good!

What is a latke?

A Latke is a fried potato pancake eaten on the Jewish holiday Hunukkah

Ingredients

  • 3 peeled potatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1/4 cup of matzo meal
  • 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • Vegetable, peanut, or olive oil

Step 1

Grate the potatoes and onions, and then place the grated veggies over a cheesecloth and wring to extract moisture

Step 2

put the shredded veggies in a bowl, add egg, the baking powder, salt, and matzo meal. Stir well until ingredients are combined

step 3

In a large skillet over medium heat, add 1/8 inch layer of your oil until shimmering. Add 1/4 cup mounds of the mixture you made, and flatten with a spatula to make a pattie shape

step 4

cook until golden brown for 4-7 minutes, flip and repeat on the other side

Serve!

transfer the latkes to a towel to drain and then serve them hot. Make sure to add either apple sauce or sour cream to dip them in!
